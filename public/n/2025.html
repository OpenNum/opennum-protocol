How OpenNum Works
No contracts. No gas. Just Bitcoin.
Step 1
Own a Bitcoin Ordinal inscription. The inscription number is your permanent identity anchor.
Step 2
Sign a registration message with your wallet's secp256k1 key. Pure off-chain. No transaction required.
Step 3
The OpenNum indexer maps #2025 to your wallet. Anyone can resolve, verify, and pay you by number.
