OpenNum is built for normal people to use Bitcoin identity without reading protocol docs. The protocol stays underneath; the number becomes the interface.

Connect Unisat, choose an inscription you own, and sign once. No on-chain registration transaction.

3
Trade the identity asset

The identity follows the inscription. Trade it on Satflow, then the new owner re-registers.
